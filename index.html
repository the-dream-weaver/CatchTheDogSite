Catch That Dog

Move your mouse toward the dog. It notices. It panics. It relocates. Your job is to click it anyway.

Caught 0
Dodges 0
Mood Suspicious

Game Arena

Sneak in from an angle. Fast, sloppy lunges only make it worse.

How To Win

  • Move slowly and stop overreacting.
  • Try cornering it instead of charging straight in.
  • After a lot of dodges, it gets briefly overconfident.
